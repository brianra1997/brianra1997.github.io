Design Philosophy

My name is Brian Ra and I am an Interction Designer from the metro Atlanta area. For those unfamiliar with Interaction Design, it is designing the way users interact with products. In simple terms, my goal is to make a product as easy to use as possible. My works were created while I studied at Kennesaw State University. My projects focused on implmenting design principles such as Goal Directed Design as well as Lean UX.

Here are some of my works

During my time at KSU, I collaborated with my peers to create pjojects using design processes such as Goal Directed Design (GDD) and Lean Ux. These processes include user interface design, prototyping, sitemapping, storytelling, modeling, usability testing, and mock up creation.
