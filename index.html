Design Philosophy

My name is Brian Ra and I am an Interction Designer from the metro Atlanta area. For those unfamiliar with Interaction Design, it is designing the way users interact with products. In simple terms, my goal is to make a product as easy to use as possible. My design philosophy starts with truly understnading the users' goals, wants, and needs to create a design that will stand out amongst my competition. Putting the user first is my number one priority. This is achieved through thorough research, interviews, communication, and most importantly, empathy.
